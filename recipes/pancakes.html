Easy Pancakes

Description

A simple pancake recipe that takes the stress out of mornings. With just a few ingredients, youâ€™ll have fluffy, delicious pancakes on the table in minutes. Perfect for busy days when you want a no-fuss breakfast that still tastes amazing!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • B2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg, beaten

Steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients.
  2. Add the wet ingredients and mix.
  3. Pour or ladle the batter onto the oiled griddle or pan.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook on the other side.
